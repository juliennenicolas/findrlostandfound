The problem

Manual logbooks and drop-off boxes mean items get scattered across offices, records go undocumented, and students are rarely notified when something of theirs turns up.

The system

Findr centralizes every report into one MySQL-backed database, with role-based accounts for students, staff, and admins, and full CRUD support for every record.

The result

Faster searching, verified claims, and reporting tools that give the school real visibility into recovery rates and unclaimed items.

What's inside

One system, every step of the process

From the moment an item is reported to the moment it's claimed, Findr keeps the record straight.

Smart search & filtering

Look up items by name, category, location, date, or reference number in seconds.

Claim verification

Students submit proof descriptions; staff review and approve directly in the system.

Role-based accounts

Separate, secure access for students, lost-and-found staff, and system admins.
