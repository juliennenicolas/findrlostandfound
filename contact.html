Helpful details

Before sending a message

Adding complete details makes it easier for staff to check the database and verify item ownership.

Describe the item clearly

Include color, brand, size, unique marks, accessories, or anything that can help confirm ownership.

Add the location

Mention where the item was lost or found, such as the library, classroom, hallway, cafeteria, or office.

Include the date

Provide the date and approximate time so staff can narrow down matching records faster.
