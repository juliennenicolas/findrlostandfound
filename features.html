Features & services

Everything the front office needs, minus the logbook.

Findr covers the full lifecycle of a lost item — from the moment it's reported to the moment it's safely back with its owner.

Centralized item records

Every lost or found item is logged with a name, description, category, exact campus location, and date — all in one database instead of scattered offices.

Smart search & filtering

Search by item name, category, date, location, status, or reference number to find a match in seconds rather than checking office to office.

Claim submission & verification

Students submit claims online with a proof description; staff review and approve or reject them, replacing verbal disputes and paper forms.

Role-based accounts

Secure logins for students, lost-and-found staff, and admins — each role sees only the tools and records relevant to them.

Reports & analytics

Generate summaries of recovery rates, unclaimed items, and peak loss periods to support better decisions by school administration.

Secure relational database

Six connected tables — users, items, categories, locations, claims, and reports — support full create, read, update, and delete operations.

File maintenance

What staff can do with every record

Standard file-maintenance operations, available across items, accounts, and claims.

Add

Log new lost item reports, found item records, user accounts, and claim requests as they come in.

Edit & update

Update item details, locations, dates, status, and claimant information as a case progresses.

Delete

Remove incorrect or duplicate entries, while valid records are saved straight to the database.

Search & filter

Find records by name, category, date, location, status, or reference number.

Backup & restore

Protect records from accidental loss with regular backups and a restore path.

Archive & report

Generate reports on lost, found, claimed, and unclaimed items, and archive old records for reference.

Who it's for

Built around three roles

Student / Faculty

Report & claim

Report a lost item, search found items, and submit a claim request with supporting details.

Lost-and-Found Staff

Record & verify

Log found items, update item status, and verify claims before marking an item as returned.

System Admin

Manage & report

Manage accounts, monitor records across the system, and generate reports for the school.
